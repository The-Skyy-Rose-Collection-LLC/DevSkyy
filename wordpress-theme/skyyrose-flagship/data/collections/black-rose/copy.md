## Origin

Black Rose was born from Oakland's specific gravity — not the Bay, Oakland. Deep East, where beauty doesn't ask permission; it forces through the cracks. Corey named it for a posture before he named it for a flower. The collection launched in 2021 as part of the three-chapter debut alongside Signature and Love Hurts.

The founding premise, verbatim from `collection-content.php`:

> "A black rose is a posture. Not a flower — a conviction. In Oakland, where concrete is the only soil that matters, beauty doesn't ask for permission. It forces its way through the cracks."

Press recognition came external — Maxim (2023), San Francisco Post (2024), Best Bay Area Clothing Line Award (2024) — but Black Rose's identity predates and supersedes all of it. The collection is armor. The recognition is acknowledgment, not origin.

## Voice & Mood

Declarative. No qualifiers. No softening. The voice says what is and moves on — Oakland taught that register. Not aggressive, not performative, just resolved.

Hero tagline (verbatim, live site): **"The beauty of the color black through the rose and high-end fashion design."**

Founder quote in `collection-content.php`:

> "Where I'm from, black isn't a color — it's armor. Every kid on my block knew that. Black Rose is that truth made into fabric. You don't wear it to stand out. You wear it because you already stood up."

Founder pull-quote from `template-parts/collection/founder-pullquote.php` (marked "Verbatim from brand-DNA"):

> "If you asked me four years ago, I never would have thought I'd be here. I had no drive, lost it all, a baby on the way, and was broke. But we knew we had to get it by any means necessary."

Mood board peers from `knowledge-base/seed/from-interview.md`: KITH, Palm Angels, FOG Eternal. Anti-references: no blue, no clichés, no dry reveals.
