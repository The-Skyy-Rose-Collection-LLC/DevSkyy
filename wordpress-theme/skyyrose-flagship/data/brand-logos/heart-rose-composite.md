# Heart-Rose Composite — Canonical Reference

The Love Hurts "heart-and-rose" mark — a **red rose rising from a broken,
thorn-wrapped heart**, without the "Love Hurts" wordmark. Used as the
embroidered thigh mark on the Love Hurts joggers (`lh-002`, `lh-006`).

> Founder-confirmed 2026-05-29 (Corey provided the canonical art). This mark
> **includes the rose** — earlier text in `love-hurts-logo.md` that described it
> as "heart-and-thorns without roses" was incomplete. The reference image at
> `image_path` is authoritative.

## Visual Description

A vertical tattoo / comic-illustration lockup, hard black ink outlines, read
top-to-bottom as rose → heart:

### Rose (top)
- One **large open red rose** bloom, center-top — layered petals in bright red
  with darker-red tonal shading and black ink edges.
- A **smaller partially-open rose / bud** to the upper-right of the main bloom.
- **Green leaves and stems** fan out behind and to the sides of the blooms.

### Broken heart (below)
- A **bright red heart**, comic-book style — rounded top lobes, pointed bottom.
- The heart is **broken / cracked** — dark fracture lines split the surface,
  exposing a **deeper crimson interior** through the breaks.
- **Brown thorny vines** wrap around and through the heart; a band of thorns
  (crown-of-thorns) crosses the heart's middle, with sharp dark thorns visible
  along the vine length. The vines intertwine with the heart's cracks.

### Blood drips (bottom)
- **Red blood drips / splatters** fall from the bottom point of the heart, with
  a few stray splatter dots to the lower sides — like wet paint.

## Color Palette

- **Bright red** (rose petals, heart): `~#D52327` to `#E63B3B`.
- **Dark red / crimson** (heart interior, shading, drips): `~#7A1217` to `#8A1A1F`.
- **Brown** (thorny vines): `~#5C3923`.
- **Green** (rose leaves / stems): `~#1F8B3D` (kelly) with `~#0E5524` shading.
- **Black** (ink outlines): pure black.

## How techniques apply this logo

- **Embroidered** (e.g., the Love Hurts joggers thigh mark): thread version —
  red thread for rose + heart, green for leaves, brown for thorns, preserving
  the rose-above-broken-heart composition.
- **Sublimated / printed**: full-color art dye-merged or flat-ink, all colors
  and the canonical composition preserved.

## What this mark IS NOT

- NOT the full "Love Hurts" lockup — there is **NO wordmark** in this composite.
- NOT a single clean heart — the heart is intentionally broken/cracked.
- NOT rose-only or heart-only — it is the integrated rose-above-broken-heart.
- NOT pink — bright saturated red, not pastel.
