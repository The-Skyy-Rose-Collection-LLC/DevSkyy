# Black Rose Authentic Collection Patch — Canonical Reference

The "Authentic Collection" patch used on Black Rose jersey-series products.
Mimics MLB-style Authentic Collection hangtags / sleeve patches, with custom
SkyyRose detailing.

## Visual Description

A roughly rectangular / portrait-oriented patch with two halves divided by a
thin red diamond divider strip:

### Upper half — the AUTHENTIC card

- **Outer border**: thin double frame — outer line in dark navy, then a hairline
  white inset, surrounding…
- **Yellow / mustard-gold field** (the dominant background color of the upper card).
- **"BLACK ROSE"** — large dark-navy serif text arched across the top of the
  yellow card.
- Below the text, an **MLB-style silhouette logo**: a square rounded-rect with
  a navy-blue field and a red wedge on the right. Inside is a **white silhouette
  of a baseball batter at the plate** (the MLB-style "Major League Baseball"
  logo composition, but recolored / restyled for SkyyRose). The batter is
  facing right, mid-swing pose.
- A **white scroll banner** crosses below the batter logo with the word
  **"AUTHENTIC"** in dark navy text, edges of the banner accented in red.
- Below the banner, **"COLLECTION"** in large dark-navy serif text, mirroring
  the "BLACK ROSE" text at the top.

### Divider

- A horizontal strip of **red diamonds** (small diamond shapes in a row,
  roughly 5–8 visible, dark red on white).

### Lower half — the place tag

- **White / cream background**.
- **"Members only"** in italic dark serif text on the left.
- **"Yay Area, Ca"** in italic dark serif text on the right (intentional
  spelling — "Yay" not "Bay" — Bay Area pun).
- A **SkyyRose Collection script logo** centered below — cursive black "SkyyRose"
  with a small crescent moon and "Collection" wordmark beneath in a smaller
  block.
- **"Made for Kings ang Queens"** in italic dark serif text along the bottom
  (intentional spelling — "ang" not "and").

## What this patch IS NOT

- NOT the canonical Black Rose three-rose-cluster logo (that is a different
  asset at `data/brand-logos/black-rose-logo.md`).
- NOT a real MLB Authentic Collection patch — it is a SkyyRose-original design
  that *references* MLB's visual language as a stylistic choice.

## How techniques apply this patch

This patch is typically rendered as a **woven or embroidered patch** sewn
onto the jersey. The intricate text + silhouette + multi-color frame mean it
is rarely embossed or printed flat — woven gives the cleanest text legibility.

- **embroidered-patch**: Patch backing fabric with embroidered text + figures.
  Most common application on jerseys.
- **woven-label**: Smaller hangtag-style applications.
- **printed / sublimated**: Possible for fabric-back applications, retains
  full multi-color fidelity.
