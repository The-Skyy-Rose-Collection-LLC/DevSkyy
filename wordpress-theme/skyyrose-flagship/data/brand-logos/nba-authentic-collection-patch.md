# NBA Authentic Collection Patch — Canonical Reference

The NBA-themed Authentic Collection patch used on the Black Rose basketball
jersey (br-010 The Bay). Mirrors the MLB and NFL Authentic Collection patches
in layout and palette but swaps the central sport-league emblem.

## Visual Description

A roughly rectangular / portrait-oriented patch with two halves divided by a
thin red diamond divider strip:

### Upper half — the AUTHENTIC card

- **Outer border**: thin double frame — outer line in dark navy, then a hairline
  white inset, surrounding…
- **Yellow / mustard-gold field** (the dominant background color of the upper
  card).
- **"BLACK ROSE"** — large dark-navy serif text arched across the top of the
  yellow card.
- Below the text, the **NBA league logo**: the canonical Jerry-West-silhouette
  basketball-player composition — a vertical-elongated rectangular badge with
  a red field on the left, a blue field on the right, and the white silhouette
  of a basketball player dribbling, centered. White "NBA" lettering at the
  bottom of the badge.
- A **white scroll banner** crosses below the badge with the word
  **"AUTHENTIC"** in dark navy text, edges of the banner accented in red.
- Below the banner, **"COLLECTION"** in large dark-navy serif text, mirroring
  the "BLACK ROSE" text at the top.

### Divider

- A horizontal strip of **red diamonds** (small diamond shapes in a row,
  roughly 9 visible, dark red on white).

### Lower half — the place tag

- **White / cream background**.
- **"Property Of"** in italic dark-serif text on the left.
- A **greyscale Black Rose three-rose-cluster** illustration centered (the
  canonical rose-cluster art with the cloud at base).
- **"Black Is Beautiful Series"** in bright red script across the lower-mid
  area.
- **"Skyy Rose Co"** in italic dark-serif text along the bottom-right.

## What this patch IS NOT

- NOT the MLB Authentic Collection patch (different silhouette, different
  footer copy).
- NOT the NFL Authentic Collection patch (different shield).
- NOT the Hockey Championship patch (greyscale palette, no yellow).

## How techniques apply this patch

- **embroidered-patch**: Most common on jerseys — woven backing with
  embroidered figure + text.
- **woven-label**: For smaller hangtag-style applications.
- **printed / sublimated**: Possible for fabric-back applications.
