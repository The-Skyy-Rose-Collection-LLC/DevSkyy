# Hockey Championship Patch — Canonical Reference

The hockey-themed counterpart to the Authentic Collection patch, used on the
Black Rose hockey jersey (br-011 The Rose). Distinct from the MLB / NFL / NBA
Authentic Collection patches by its **greyscale / silver palette** and a
different layout (no yellow upper field, no red footer copy block).

## Visual Description

A portrait-oriented patch in a **grey / silver tonal palette** only:

### Outer border

- Thin double frame — outer line in deep navy / black, hairline inset, then
  another navy line — wrapping a silver/grey field.

### Upper half — the championship card

- **Light silver-grey field** (the dominant background color).
- **"BLACK ROSE"** — large dark-navy serif text arched across the top.
- Below the text, the **Hockey Championship shield emblem**: a black shield
  shape with "HOCKEY" in white block-letters across the upper portion,
  "CHAMPIONSHIP" in white inside a horizontal banner across the middle, three
  small white stars at the bottom of the shield, **two crossed hockey sticks**
  passing diagonally behind the shield (their white-taped shafts visible above
  and below the shield), and a **black hockey puck** sitting at the top of
  the shield where the sticks cross.
- Below the shield, **"COLLECTION"** in large dark-navy serif text, mirroring
  the "BLACK ROSE" text at the top.

### Divider

- A horizontal strip of **dark grey diamonds** (small diamond shapes in a
  row, ~9 visible, dark grey-on-grey for low-contrast tonal effect — distinct
  from the red diamonds on the MLB/NFL/NBA patches).

### Lower half

- **Solid silver-grey field continues** below the diamond divider.
- The lower half is intentionally **uncluttered** — no "Property Of" copy,
  no rose-cluster illustration, no "Black Is Beautiful Series" wordmark, no
  "Skyy Rose Co" copy. The hockey patch keeps the lower field clean as part
  of its tonal-monochrome aesthetic. (This is the key visual difference vs.
  the MLB/NFL/NBA Authentic Collection patches.)

## What this patch IS NOT

- NOT the MLB / NFL / NBA Authentic Collection patches (those have a
  yellow upper field, red diamond divider, and a populated white lower-half
  footer block — none of which this patch has).
- NOT a multi-color patch — the entire mark is greyscale / silver / black /
  white.
- NOT a small woven label (this is a full sleeve / hip patch).

## How techniques apply this patch

- **embroidered-patch**: Most common on jerseys — woven backing with
  embroidered shield + sticks + lettering. The grey/silver tonal palette
  suits matte-thread embroidery cleanly.
- **woven-label**: For smaller hangtag-style applications.
- **printed / sublimated**: Possible for fabric-back applications, retains
  the silver-grey gradient and shield shape.
