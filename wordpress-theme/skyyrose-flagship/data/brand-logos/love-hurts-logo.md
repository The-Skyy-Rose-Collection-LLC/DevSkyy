# Love Hurts Logo — Canonical Reference

The Love Hurts collection's primary lockup mark. Distinct from the Black Rose
logo by its red color palette, broken-heart imagery, and graffiti-style script
lettering. Used on every Love Hurts collection product.

## Visual Description

A two-element composition combining a stylized "Love Hurts" wordmark with a
broken-heart-and-thorns illustration:

### Wordmark — "Love Hurts"

- Two cursive script words stacked vertically: **"Love"** above **"Hurts"**.
- Letters in **bright saturated red** with a **white outline / inline highlight**,
  finished with a **dark red drop-shadow / outline** for depth.
- Graffiti-style cursive — bold, swooping, with hand-drawn imperfections.
- **Red drips / splatters** flow downward from the bottom of the word "Hurts"
  (and below the L of "Love"), like wet paint.

### Heart-and-thorns illustration

- A **bright red heart** to the upper right of the wordmark, drawn in
  comic-book / illustrated style with hard ink edges.
- The heart is **broken** — multiple **dark cracks / fractures** run across
  the surface, exposing the deeper crimson interior through the breaks.
- **Brown/dark thorny vines** wrap around and through the heart, with sharp
  dark thorns clearly visible along the vine length.
- The vines pierce / wrap through the cracks, intertwining with the heart's
  fractures.

### Composition

- The heart sits in the **upper-right** of the lockup, the wordmark fills the
  **lower-left**. The two elements overlap slightly so the lockup reads as
  a single integrated graphic.
- Overall composition is **roughly square** with a slight white halo / outline
  around the entire mark.

## Color Palette

- **Red** (heart, lettering): bright saturated red (~`#D52327` to `#E63B3B`).
- **Dark red** (drop shadow, heart shadow tones): `~#7A1217`.
- **Brown** (thorny vines): `~#5C3923`.
- **White** (lettering inline highlight, halo): pure white.

## What this logo IS NOT

- NOT the Black Rose three-rose-cluster (different collection — `data/brand-logos/black-rose-logo.md`).
- NOT a single heart or single rose — it is a multi-element lockup
  with the wordmark + the broken heart + thorny vines.
- NOT pink — the Love Hurts palette is bright saturated red, not pastel.
- NOT clean / unbroken — the heart is intentionally cracked / broken.
