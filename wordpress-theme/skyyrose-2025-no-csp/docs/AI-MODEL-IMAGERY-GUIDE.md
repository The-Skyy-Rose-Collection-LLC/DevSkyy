# SkyyRose AI Model Imagery Guide
## Professional Product Photography with AI-Generated Models

**Purpose**: Generate 20-30 high-quality product photos featuring AI models wearing authentic SkyyRose brand assets.

---

## Brand Visual Identity

### Core Aesthetic
- **Black Rose**: Dark, gothic, futuristic. Dramatic lighting, black/white/metallic palette, sharp edges
- **Love Hurts**: Romantic rebellion. Soft heart meets sharp edge, pink/rose tones, distressed details
- **Signature**: Timeless luxury. Gold accents, minimal, architectural, premium cuts

### Color Codes
```
Black Rose: #8B0000 (dark red), #000000 (black), #C0C0C0 (metallic silver)
Love Hurts: #B76E79 (dusty rose), #FFB6C1 (light pink), #2D1A1D (deep burgundy)
Signature: #D4AF37 (gold), #000000 (black), #FFFFFF (white)
```

### Photography Style
- **Lighting**: Dramatic, high-contrast for Black Rose; soft, romantic for Love Hurts; clean, minimal for Signature
- **Setting**: Urban environments (Oakland streets, warehouses, rooftops), luxury interiors, abstract backgrounds
- **Models**: Diverse representation, confident poses, editorial quality
- **Mood**: Bold, unapologetic, luxurious, emotionally resonant

## Technical Specifications

### Image Requirements
- **Resolution**: Minimum 2000x3000px (2:3 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: PNG or high-quality JPG (90%+quality)
- **Color Space**: sRGB
- **File Size**: 2-5MB per image

### Composition Guidelines
- **Rule of Thirds**: Position model's eyes on upper third line
- **Negative Space**: Leave room for text overlays in product pages
- **Focus**: Sharp focus on garment details, model's face can have slight blur
- **Framing**: Full body (for lookbook), mid-shot (for product detail), close-up (for textures)

---

## Post-Processing Workflow

### 1. AI Generation
- Generate 3-5 variations per prompt
- Select best 1-2 for refinement

### 2. Photoshop Touchup
- **Color Correction**: Ensure brand colors are accurate (#8B0000, #B76E79, #D4AF37)
- **Product Enhancement**: Sharpen garment details, adjust fabric texture
- **Skin Retouching**: Subtle smoothing while maintaining texture
- **Lighting Adjustments**: Enhance dramatic lighting for Black Rose, soften for Love Hurts

### 3. Brand Asset Integration
- Add authentic product details (logos, embroidery, prints)
- Ensure rose motifs are visible and well-defined
- Match product photos to actual inventory

### 4. Final Export
- **Web Optimized**: 1200x1800px, JPG 85% quality, sRGB
- **Print Ready**: 3000x4500px, PNG, Adobe RGB
- **Thumbnails**: 400x600px, JPG 80% quality

## Ethical AI Use Guidelines

1. **Disclosure**: Mark all AI-generated images with metadata
2. **No Deepfakes**: Never use AI to replicate real people without consent
3. **Diversity**: Actively ensure representation across all protected classes
4. **Quality**: Only use images that match professional photography standards
5. **Brand Alignment**: Every image must embody SkyyRose values: bold, luxurious, emotionally authentic
