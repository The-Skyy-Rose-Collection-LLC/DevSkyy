# SkyyRose Brand Voice Guide

**Purpose**: This document defines the voice, tone, and language standards for all customer-facing content.

---

## Brand Identity

### Core Values
- **Luxury Streetwear**: High-end fashion with urban roots
- **Oakland Authenticity**: Genuine Bay Area heritage without stereotypes
- **Boutique-Ready Presentation**: Every touchpoint feels curated and premium

### Brand Name Rules
| Correct | Incorrect |
|---------|-----------|
| SkyyRose | Skyy Rose (two words) |
| SkyyRose | skyy rose (lowercase) |

## Language Guidelines

### DO Use
- "Curated collection"
- "Elevated streetwear"
- "Premium quality"
- "Exclusive design"
- "Limited release"
- "Boutique experience"
- "Crafted with intention"
- "Oakland heritage"

### DO NOT Use
- ❌ "Cheap" or "affordable" (use "accessible" if needed)
- ❌ "Sale" or "discount" (use "exclusive offer" sparingly)
- ❌ "Clearance" (never)
- ❌ Hyphy slang ("hella", "yadadamean", "go dumb")
- ❌ Generic streetwear clichés ("drip", "fire", "bussin")
- ❌ Urgency tactics ("Buy now!", "Limited time only!")

## Product Description Templates

### Standard Product
```
[Product Name]

[One-sentence brand story or design inspiration]

[Key features - 2-3 bullet points]
• [Material/quality detail]
• [Design element]
• [Fit/comfort detail]

[Care instructions if applicable]
```

### Example
```
Oakland Skyline Hoodie

Inspired by the city lights that never sleep, this heavyweight hoodie
captures the essence of Oakland's urban landscape.

• Premium 400 GSM French terry cotton
• Embroidered skyline detail on back panel
• Relaxed fit with ribbed cuffs and hem

Machine wash cold, tumble dry low.
```

## Social Media Voice

### Instagram
- Sophisticated but approachable
- High-quality imagery focus
- Minimal caption text, let visuals speak
- Hashtags: #SkyyRose #OaklandLuxury #ElevatedStreet

### Email
- Personal, like a note from a trusted friend
- Subject lines: Intriguing, never clickbait
- Always address by name when available

## Customer Service Tone

### Response Framework
1. **Acknowledge**: Validate the customer's experience
2. **Address**: Solve the issue with clarity
3. **Appreciate**: Thank them for being part of SkyyRose

### Example Responses

**Complaint**:
> "Thank you for bringing this to our attention. We take quality seriously
> at SkyyRose, and we want to make this right. [Solution]. We appreciate
> you being part of our community."

**Question**:
> "Great question! [Clear answer]. If you need anything else, we're here
> for you. Welcome to SkyyRose."

## Prohibited Language Patterns

### Never Use These Phrases
- "Best deal ever"
- "You won't believe..."
- "Act fast"
- "Going, going, gone"
- "Steal this look"
- "Budget-friendly"

### Cultural Sensitivity
- Avoid appropriating Oakland culture for marketing
- Represent authentically, not stereotypically
- When featuring Oakland, focus on artistry, innovation, community

## Quality Checklist

Before publishing any content:

- [ ] Brand name spelled correctly (SkyyRose)
- [ ] Domain is .co (not .com)
- [ ] No discount/sale language
- [ ] No hyphy slang or clichés
- [ ] Tone is elevated but accessible
- [ ] Oakland references are respectful
- [ ] Imagery matches luxury positioning
