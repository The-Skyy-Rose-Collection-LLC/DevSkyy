**TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION: PANELLED JOGGERS WITH EMBROIDERED ROSE MOTIF**

This specification details two color variations of the same jogger design.

---

**GARMENT A: BLACK / WHITE COLORWAY**
(As shown in Image 1)

1.  **GARMENT TYPE:** Athletic Joggers / Track Pants.
2.  **BASE COLOR:** Jet Black (dominant body panels).
3.  **SECONDARY COLORS:**
    *   Pure White (side and inner leg panels, drawstring).
    *   Crimson Red (roses in graphic embroidery).
    *   Forest Green (leaves and stems in graphic embroidery).
    *   Metallic Silver (zipper teeth, zipper pull tabs, drawstring eyelets).
4.  **FIT:**
    *   **Silhouette:** Modern athletic fit, relaxed through the hip and thigh, tapering significantly from the knee down to a snug ankle opening.
    *   **Length:** Full-length, designed to reach the ankle.
    *   **Proportions:** Standard mid-rise waistband.
5.  **GRAPHICS:**
    *   **Location:** Upper right thigh/hip area, positioned approximately 2.5 inches (6.35 cm) below the top edge of the waistband and 1.5 inches (3.81 cm) inboard from the side seam (black panel edge). The motif is oriented slightly diagonally, angled downwards from the left.
    *   **Exact Size Relative to Garment:** Approximately 5.5 inches (13.97 cm) in height and 5 inches (12.7 cm) in width at its widest points.
    *   **Content:** An embroidered cluster of three fully bloomed roses with associated leaves and stems. The roses are depicted with varying petal structures, indicating different stages of bloom, with visible thorn details on the stems.
    *   **Colors:** Roses are rendered in multiple shades of crimson red, utilizing satin and fill stitches for depth and texture. Leaves and stems are embroidered in a deep forest green, with minor tonal variations for realism.
    *   **Style:** Detailed, high-density embroidery with a slightly raised, tactile texture, reminiscent of traditional tattoo-flash or botanical illustration.
6.  **TEXT:** None visible.
7.  **LOGOS & BRANDING:** None visible.
8.  **HARDWARE:**
    *   **Drawstring:** Flat woven cord, 0.4 inches (1 cm) wide, pure white. Exits through two metallic eyelets at the center front waistband.
    *   **Eyelets:** Two round, metallic silver eyelets, approximately 0.3 inches (0.75 cm) in diameter, positioned equidistantly on either side of the drawstring exit on the waistband.
    *   **Leg Zippers:** Two functional coil zippers, one on the outer seam of each leg cuff.
        *   **Teeth:** Metallic silver finish.
        *   **Pull Tab:** Small, elongated rectangular pull tab, metallic silver finish.
        *   **Length:** Approximately 7.5 inches (19.05 cm) long, extending upwards from the bottom hem.
9.  **FABRIC TEXTURE:**
    *   **Sheen:** Smooth, uniform surface with a subtle, low-to-medium sheen, characteristic of a brushed polyester tricot or similar performance athletic fabric.
    *   **Thickness:** Medium-weight knit fabric, offering comfort and durability without excessive bulk.
    *   **Ribbing:** None visible on main body.
    *   **Mesh:** None visible.
    *   **Sherpa:** None visible.
    *   **Surface:** A soft hand feel is implied by the fabric's drape and subtle sheen.
10. **SPECIAL DETAILS:**
    *   **Waistband:** Fully elasticated waistband, approximately 1.5 inches (3.81 cm) tall, crafted from the same Jet Black body fabric, with an internal drawstring for adjustable fit.
    *   **Side Panels:** A distinct, continuous Pure White panel runs vertically down the outer side seam of each leg, extending from the top of the waistband to the bottom hem.
    *   **Inner Leg Panels:** A complementary Pure White panel runs along the inner leg seam of each leg, from the crotch point down to the bottom hem. The white side and inner leg panels converge at the crotch area, creating a visually distinct Y-shaped white element on the front of the leg.
    *   **Pockets:** Two slanted, open-entry side pockets, one on each hip. The pocket openings are seamlessly integrated into the Jet Black main body panel, adjacent to the white side stripe.
    *   **Cuffs:** Cleanly hemmed ankle cuffs, featuring the functional zipper on the outer seam to allow for adjustable opening width and ease of dressing.
    *   **Seams:** All major construction seams (panel seams, pocket attachments, waistband attachment) are executed with precision, likely using flatlock or overlock stitching for enhanced durability, comfort, and a clean interior finish typical of high-quality athletic apparel.
