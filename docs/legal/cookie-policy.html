1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website places on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) when you visit. They are widely used to make websites work properly, to remember your preferences, and to provide information to site owners about how their pages are used.

Beyond traditional cookies, we and our partners may also use related technologies:

  • Pixels (web beacons). Tiny invisible images or snippets of JavaScript embedded in a page or email. When your browser loads the pixel, the provider receives information such as your IP address, browser type, the page you visited, and the time. We use pixels for advertising measurement (e.g., the TikTok pixel).
  • Local storage & session storage. Browser-based storage that works similarly to cookies but is not automatically sent to servers with every request. We and WooCommerce may use local storage to preserve cart state and site preferences between sessions without setting a traditional cookie.
  • Session identifiers. Temporary identifiers held in memory for the duration of your browser session; they expire when you close the tab or window.

Throughout this policy the word cookies refers collectively to all of these technologies unless otherwise specified.

2. How we use cookies

We use cookies for four broad purposes:

  • Operating the store. Certain cookies are essential for the Site to function. Without them, key features such as the shopping cart, secure checkout, and login sessions cannot work.
  • Remembering the cart. WooCommerce sets cookies to persist the items in your cart across pages and browser sessions so you do not lose your selection.
  • Analytics. We use cookies to understand how visitors use the Site — which pages are most popular, how long people spend, and where they come from — so we can improve the shopping experience.
  • Advertising. We use advertising pixels to measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns and to serve you relevant advertisements on third-party platforms such as TikTok. This type of activity may constitute "sharing" personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising under California law. See Section 4 and the Privacy Policy for your opt-out rights.

SkyyRose LLC does not sell personal information in the traditional sense, but this type of sharing is treated as an opt-out right under California law.

To exercise your right to opt out of sharing, please see the Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information section of our Privacy Policy, or use the opt-out tools described in Section 5 below.

5. How to manage or disable cookies

You have several options for controlling cookies. Note that disabling Strictly Necessary cookies will prevent the shopping cart and checkout from functioning correctly.

Browser controls

Most browsers let you view, block, or delete cookies through their settings. Instructions for common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Blocking all cookies in your browser will affect many websites beyond skyyrose.co. Consider using more targeted controls below.

Provider opt-outs

  • Google Analytics opt-out. Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent your data from being used by Google Analytics across all sites. Alternatively, adjust your Google account's Ad Settings to limit personalized advertising.
  • TikTok ad settings. If you have a TikTok account, manage your advertising preferences in the TikTok app under Settings & Privacy > Ads Preferences. You can also opt out of TikTok interest-based ads at TikTok Cookie Policy.
  • Industry opt-out tools. Visit the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out to opt out of interest-based advertising from participating companies.

Global Privacy Control (GPC)

We honor the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal. If your browser or browser extension transmits a GPC signal when you visit skyyrose.co, we will treat it as a request to opt out of the sale and sharing of your personal information, consistent with California law. Note that GPC is a browser-level signal; it applies to the specific browser and device you are using and does not carry over to other devices or browsers.

Important limitation

Opting out of advertising and analytics cookies does not remove all cookies from your browser. Strictly Necessary cookies — those required for the cart, checkout, and site security — will continue to operate. Disabling them through your browser settings may cause the Site to malfunction.
