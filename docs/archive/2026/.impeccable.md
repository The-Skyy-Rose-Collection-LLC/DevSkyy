## Design Context

### Users
- **Primary User:** The Founder/CEO.
- **Context:** Orchestrating tasks, managing the AI agent workforce, and overseeing the luxury e-commerce operations.
- **Job to be Done:** Seamlessly translating creative and operational directives from the dashboard directly to the customer-facing SkyyRose website.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice:** "Luxury Grows from Concrete."
- **Tone:** A dual-sided personality. A mix of the high-end, editorial refinement ("Cinematic, Luxury, Uncompromising") combined with the raw, grounded reality of the streets ("Organic, Technical, Warm").
- **Vibe:** Expensive but grounded. Not sterile or corporate.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Customer Site (Phase 1 Priority):** High-end editorial magazine. Cinematic, image-led, with wide typography, deep contrasts, and slow, deliberate reveals. 
- **Agent Dashboard (Phase 2):** A technical yet luxurious command center.
- **Theme:** Predominantly Dark mode, utilizing the brand's metallic and jewel-tone accents (Rose Gold, Silver, Crimson, Gold).
- **Anti-patterns:** No generic SaaS glassmorphism. No glowing neon gradients. No "AI-slop" visual tropes.

### Design Principles
1. **Pacing is a Privilege:** Lean into a "Slow UI" for the customer site. Use cinematic transitions, exponential easing, and deliberate scroll reveals to make the experience feel expensive and intentional.
2. **Editorial Typography:** Enforce strict typographic hierarchy using the established brand fonts (Cinzel, Playfair Display). Treat web pages like high-fashion magazine spreads—never let text wrap endlessly.
3. **Intentional Contrast:** Balance the "Concrete" (raw, utilitarian layouts, rigid bento grids) with the "Luxury" (rich textures, perfect spacing, metallic brand accents).
4. **Command & Control:** When shifting to the dashboard, prioritize high-signal density and decisive orchestration, but never sacrifice the brand's aesthetic standards.
