# Theme Research

You are triggering the Experience Researcher to gather deep insights for a theme concept.

## Research Process

Use the **experience-researcher** agent to conduct comprehensive research.

### 1. Understand the Brief

Clarify what we're researching:
- What industry/niche?
- What style are we exploring?
- Any specific competitors to analyze?
- What problems are we solving?

### 2. Research Areas

**Design Inspiration**
- Search Awwwards for award-winning e-commerce sites
- Find Dribbble shots for UI inspiration
- Explore Behance for complete case studies
- Look at leading brands in the niche

**Competitor Analysis**
- Identify 3-5 direct competitors
- Analyze their strengths
- Find their weaknesses
- Identify opportunities

**User Psychology**
- How do customers shop in this category?
- What builds trust?
- What drives conversions?
- What are pain points?

**Technical Trends**
- Current web technology trends
- Animation patterns
- 3D/immersive trends
- Performance best practices

### 3. Use Research Sources

- **WebSearch**: Find latest trends and examples
- **WebFetch**: Analyze specific sites and articles
- **Context7**: Get official documentation for technologies

- Be thorough - research is the foundation
