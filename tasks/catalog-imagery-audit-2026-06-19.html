How front_model_image reaches the shopper

The theme PHP reads the CSV directly (inc/product-catalog.php:104) and uses front_model_image as the primary holo-card image, overriding the WooCommerce featured image (template-parts/product-card-holo.php:42-44). Also consumed by inc/woocommerce.php:608, inc/immersive-product-adapter.php:63-64, inc/product-catalog-display.php:55. WooCommerce is not the image authority — the CSV is. A CSV cell pointing at a file that won't ship = a broken card on the live site.
