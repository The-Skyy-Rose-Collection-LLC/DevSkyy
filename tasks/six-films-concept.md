## Brand-DNA spine
Not six ads — one journey in chapters: **Corey Foster**, single father in Oakland, rock bottom → legacy.
The collections already ARE the arc: Origin → Refusal → Grief → Heir; the house film is where they land.
Pillars: Oakland/The Town · gender-neutral by default · Black-owned · born from struggle (real, not marketing) ·
family at the core · quality not fast-fashion. Positioning: "Where Bay Area authenticity meets high-fashion aesthetics."
Guardrail: collection NAMES appear as brand-script lockup IMAGES on screen, never typed.
