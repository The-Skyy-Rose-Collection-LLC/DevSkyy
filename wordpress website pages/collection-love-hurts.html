The Heart of the Collection

LOVE HURTS was born from the understanding that pain and beauty are inseparable. This collection transforms emotional experiences into wearable art, celebrating the strength found in vulnerability.

"Every scar tells a story. Every heartbreak builds character. This collection is for those who have loved deeply and emerged stronger."

Inspired by the rose gold hues of twilight and the deep burgundy of aged wine, each piece carries the weight of authentic emotion while maintaining the luxurious standards that define SkyyRose.
