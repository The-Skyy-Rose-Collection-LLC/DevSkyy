## Retargeting Framework

### Funnel Stages
| Stage | Audience | Window | Message | Budget Share |
|-------|----------|--------|---------|-------------|
| 1. Awareness | Visited any page | 1-30 days | Brand story, collection overview | 30% |
| 2. Consideration | Viewed product page | 1-14 days | Specific product they viewed + social proof | 40% |
| 3. Intent | Added to cart | 1-7 days | Cart reminder + urgency + free shipping offer | 30% |

### Exclusions (Critical)
- Always exclude purchasers from prospecting and retargeting
- Exclude cart retargeting from people who already completed checkout
- Exclude welcome flow subscribers from aggressive retargeting for 7 days

### Frequency Caps
- Prospecting: Max 3 impressions/person/week
- Retargeting (consideration): Max 5/week
- Retargeting (intent): Max 7/week (urgency justifies higher frequency)

## Budget Calculator

### Starting Budget Formula
```
Monthly revenue target: $[X]
Average order value (AOV): $65 (blended across products)
Target ROAS: 3x (return $3 for every $1 spent)
Monthly ad budget = Revenue target / Target ROAS

Example: $5,000 revenue target / 3x ROAS = $1,667/month ad spend
Daily: ~$55/day across all platforms
```

### Platform Budget Split (Launch Phase)
| Platform | Allocation | Daily ($55 total) | Why |
|----------|-----------|-------------------|-----|
| Meta (IG/FB) | 50% | $27.50 | Best for fashion discovery + retargeting |
| TikTok | 30% | $16.50 | Gen-Z reach, organic amplification |
| Google | 20% | $11.00 | Capture search intent + brand protection |

### Testing → Scaling Decision Framework
```
Week 1-2: Test phase
- Run 3-5 creative variations per platform
- $20-30/day total
- Goal: identify winning creative + audience

Week 3-4: Optimize phase
- Kill underperformers (2x target CPA after $20 spent)
- Double down on winners
- $40-60/day total

Month 2+: Scale phase
- Increase budget 20-30% every 3-4 days on winners
- Introduce new creative every 2 weeks
- Add lookalike audiences from purchaser data
- $80-150/day total
```

## Anti-Patterns

- **DO NOT** run discount-heavy ads — SkyyRose is luxury. "20% OFF EVERYTHING" destroys brand equity
- **DO NOT** use stock photography — only SkyyRose product renders and lifestyle imagery
- **DO NOT** target too narrowly — audiences under 100K are too small for Meta's algorithm
- **DO NOT** kill ads before $20 in spend — small samples are unreliable
- **DO NOT** boost posts from the page — use Ads Manager for proper targeting and tracking
- **DO NOT** run the same creative for more than 3 weeks — refresh to combat fatigue
- **DO NOT** forget retargeting — it converts 3-5x better than cold traffic
- **DO NOT** split attention with multiple CTAs — one button, one destination per ad

## Recovery

- **High CPC / low CTR:** Creative isn't stopping the scroll. Test new hooks and visual formats.
- **Good CTR but no conversions:** Landing page problem. Check page speed, mobile UX, checkout friction.
- **Facebook ad account restricted:** Review Meta ad policies. Fashion imagery must comply with body image rules.
- **TikTok ads not spending:** Bid is too low or audience is too narrow. Broaden targeting.
- **ROAS below 2x after 30 days:** Reassess offer, funnel, or creative strategy before increasing spend.
