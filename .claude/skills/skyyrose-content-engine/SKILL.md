# SkyyRose Content Engine

## When to Use This Skill

- Planning a 30-day social content calendar
- Writing TikTok or Instagram Reels scripts
- Creating Instagram carousel slide sequences
- Generating captions for any platform
- Building hashtag sets per collection/campaign
- Repurposing one asset across platforms
- Defining content pillars and posting cadence

## 30-Day Content Calendar Template

### Weekly Rhythm
| Day | Pillar | Format | Platform Focus |
|-----|--------|--------|---------------|
| Mon | Brand Story | Carousel or static + long caption | Instagram Feed |
| Tue | Product Showcase | Reel / TikTok (15-30s) | IG Reels + TikTok |
| Wed | Culture & Community | UGC repost or community spotlight | IG Stories + X |
| Thu | Lifestyle | Styled outfit photo or mood board | IG Feed + Pinterest |
| Fri | Product Showcase | Behind-the-scenes video | TikTok + IG Reels |
| Sat | Exclusivity / Drop Hype | Countdown, teaser, or sold-out flex | IG Stories + X |
| Sun | REST or Engagement | Poll, Q&A, or community reply thread | IG Stories |

### Daily Stories (in addition to feed posts)
- 2-3 Stories per day minimum
- Mix: behind-scenes, polls, product close-ups, customer DM screenshots (with permission), repost tags

### Monthly Specials
- Week 1: "Behind the Brand" series (4 posts telling one story arc)
- Week 2: Product deep-dive (one product, 4 angles across the week)
- Week 3: Community week (all UGC, customer spotlights, reposts)
- Week 4: Drop hype or preview of next month's release

## Platform Playbooks

### Instagram
**Feed Posts:**
- Aspect: 4:5 (1080x1350) for maximum real estate
- Aesthetic: Dark/moody for Black Rose, warm/golden for Signature, red dramatic for Love Hurts
- Captions: 150-300 words, hook in first line (before "...more"), CTA at end
- Hashtags: 15-20 per post in first comment (not in caption)
- Posting time: 11am-1pm or 7-9pm PST

**Reels:**
- Duration: 15-30 seconds (algorithm favors short)
- Hook: First 1.5 seconds must stop the scroll
- Captions: Burned into video (70% of viewers watch without sound)
- Trending audio: Check Reels tab weekly for trending sounds
- CTA: "Link in bio" or "Comment DROP for early access"

**Carousels:**
- 5-7 slides optimal
- Slide 1: Hook (question, bold statement, or striking image)
- Slides 2-5: Value delivery (styling tips, collection story, product details)
- Final slide: CTA (save, share, comment, or visit link)

**Stories:**
- Use interactive stickers: polls, quizzes, question boxes, sliders
- Highlight covers: one per collection + "Behind the Scenes" + "Reviews"
- Story ads: use for retargeting with product they viewed

### TikTok
**Content Types:**
1. Product reveals (unboxing, first look)
2. Founder story snippets (personal, raw)
3. "How to style" transitions
4. Behind-the-scenes (production, packing orders)
5. Trend participation (adapt trending formats to brand)
6. "POV" format (native to platform)

**Posting:** 1-2x daily, test posting times (9am, 12pm, 6pm, 9pm)

**Duets/Stitches:** Engage with fashion content, streetwear opinions, Black culture moments

### X / Twitter
**Post Types:**
- Product drop announcements (concise, link to site)
- Founder perspective / hot takes on fashion industry
- Behind-the-scenes one-liners with photos
- Quote-tweeting customer praise
- Threads for brand story or collection deep-dives

**Voice:** More casual, more opinionated than other platforms. Oakland energy.

### Pinterest
**Board Structure:**
- "Black Rose Collection" (all BR products + styling)
- "Love Hurts Collection" (all LH products + mood)
- "Signature Collection" (all SIG products + Bay Area aesthetic)
- "Streetwear Styling" (general outfit inspo featuring SkyyRose)
- "Oakland Style" (Bay Area fashion, culture, landmarks)

**Pin Optimization:**
- Vertical format (1000x1500)
- Product pins linked to WooCommerce product pages
- Rich Pins enabled via WooCommerce + Yoast
- Descriptions: keyword-rich, 200-300 chars
- Pin 5-10 per day (mix of original + repins)

## Video Script Templates

### 15-Second Product Reveal
```
[0-1.5s] HOOK: Text overlay on black screen: "[provocative statement]"
[1.5-5s] REVEAL: Hands pulling product from branded packaging
[5-10s] DETAIL: Close-up of fabric, embroidery, or construction detail
[10-13s] WEAR: Quick cut to product being worn — confident pose
[13-15s] CTA: SkyyRose logo + "Link in bio" or "skyyrose.co"
```

### 30-Second Brand Story
```
[0-3s] HOOK: Corey in Oakland setting. Text: "[emotional statement]"
[3-10s] CONTEXT: Voice-over about the journey while showing production/products
[10-20s] PRODUCTS: Quick montage of collections — 1-2 seconds each
[20-27s] EMOTION: Corey with Skyy Rose or community moment
[27-30s] CLOSE: Tagline "Luxury Grows from Concrete" + logo
```

### 60-Second "How to Style"
```
[0-3s] HOOK: Text: "3 ways to style the [Product Name]"
[3-20s] LOOK 1: Full outfit reveal with text overlay describing the vibe
[20-37s] LOOK 2: Different context/styling, transition effect
[37-54s] LOOK 3: Most elevated look, slow reveal
[54-60s] CTA: "Which is your style? Comment 1, 2, or 3" + link
```

## Caption Formulas

### Product Launch
```
[Product name]. [One sentence about what makes it special].

[2-3 sentences about construction, material, or design story].

[Pre-order/availability info]. [Specific CTA].

[Collection hashtag set]
```

**Example:**
```
Black Rose Sherpa Jacket. Built like Oakland winter — tough on the outside, warm where it counts.

Sherpa-lined body with waxed cotton shell. Embroidered Black Rose script on the chest. Matte black hardware throughout. This isn't a fashion jacket. It's a statement that keeps you warm.

Limited to pre-order. $95. Link in bio.

#SkyyRose #BlackRoseCollection #LuxuryGrowsFromConcrete #OaklandFashion #LuxuryStreetwear
```

### Community / UGC Repost
```
[Customer name or handle] wearing the [product]. [One sentence of genuine appreciation].

[Tag/encourage others to share].
```

### Founder / Story
```
[Personal opening — specific moment or memory].

[2-3 sentences of reflection or lesson].

[Connect back to the brand or customer].

[Tagline or sign-off]
```

## Cross-Platform Repurposing Flow

```
Source Asset: 1 product drop TikTok video (30s)
    ↓
TikTok: Post native (original)
    ↓
IG Reel: Re-edit with IG-friendly text overlays + different music
    ↓
IG Feed: Extract best frame as static product photo + long caption
    ↓
IG Stories: 3-slide teaser (frame 1: hook, frame 2: product, frame 3: swipe-up CTA)
    ↓
X/Twitter: 1-line hook + product image + link to product page
    ↓
Pinterest: Vertical product image with lifestyle context → pin to collection board
    ↓
Email: Use same imagery + adapted copy in next newsletter or drop email
```

**Rule:** Each platform gets a native adaptation. Never cross-post identical content.

## Anti-Patterns

- **DO NOT** post the same thing on every platform — adapt per platform
- **DO NOT** use excessive hashtags in captions (put them in first comment on IG)
- **DO NOT** post product shots without context — every image needs a story or styling angle
- **DO NOT** ignore comments and DMs — engagement drives reach
- **DO NOT** chase every trend — only adapt trends that fit SkyyRose's aesthetic
- **DO NOT** use stock music or generic Canva templates — the brand is premium
- **DO NOT** post without burned-in captions on video — 70% of views are muted
- **DO NOT** go dark for more than 2 days — consistency builds audience trust

## Recovery

- **Running out of content ideas:** Return to the 5 pillars. Each pillar generates infinite variations.
- **Low engagement on a post:** Check the hook. If the first line/1.5 seconds doesn't stop the scroll, the content doesn't matter.
- **Trending sound doesn't fit brand:** Skip it. Forcing a trend damages authenticity.
- **No UGC to repost:** Proactively DM 5 customers who recently purchased and ask them to tag you.
- **Pinterest not getting traction:** Focus on keyword-rich descriptions and consistent daily pinning. Pinterest is a long game (3-6 months to see results).
