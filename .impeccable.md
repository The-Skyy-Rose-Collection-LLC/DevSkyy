# SkyyRose — Impeccable Design Context

> Governs the **skyyrose.co storefront** (luxury WordPress theme). The devskyy.app ops dashboard is a
> separate surface with its own utilitarian language — do not apply this context there.
> Established via `/impeccable teach`, 2026-07-10.

## Design Context

### Users
Luxury-streetwear buyers, Oakland / Bay Area rooted and culture-literate — they value heritage, craft, and
belonging over logos-for-logos'-sake. They arrive on skyyrose.co for drops: dark, immersive, unhurried. The
garment is the protagonist; the site earns the sale by making the piece feel inevitable, not by pressure.

### Brand Personality
**"Luxury Grows from Concrete."** Oakland-forged, declarative, armor. Three words: *Oakland-forged ·
declarative-armor · athletic-luxury.* Black-owned American luxury streetwear. Voice says what is and moves on —
no qualifiers, no urgency timers, no related-products clutter. Each collection keeps its own register:
Black Rose = armor / "concrete answering back"; Love Hurts = "the bloodline that raised me"; Signature =
elevated heritage; Kids Capsule = warm / playful.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **References — The Five** (locked): Kith · Oaklandish · Culture Kings · Fear of God · Palm Angels.
  Photography-led, monogram/logo-as-protagonist, civic-pride heritage, magazine-grid, disciplined palettes,
  athletic DNA underneath the luxury polish.
- **Anti-references** (locked out): European luxury-house lineage — Bottega, Rick Owens, Off-White,
  Givenchy-by-Tisci, 032c, Acne, Numéro. Wrong DNA.
- **Theme:** dark (`#0A0A0A`), per-collection accents (Rose Gold `#B76E79`, BR Silver `#C0C0C0`,
  LH Crimson `#DC143C`, SIG Gold `#D4AF37`).
- **Register:** both, per funnel stage — cinematic/immersive top-of-funnel (hero, 3D collection worlds),
  restrained editorial on shop / PDP / cart.
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.2 AA + `prefers-reduced-motion` treated as a first-class design path (the site
  runs heavy Three.js + scroll animation).

**CUT — do NOT reintroduce:** Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, Bebas Neue, Yellowtail. They pull toward
the European-serif lineage the founder locked out and appear in **no** brand lockup. The `--skyyrose-font-*`
vars are generated from `data/brand/typography.json` via `gen-design-tokens.py`; per-collection scripts live in
`data/collections/<slug>/identity.json`. All self-hosted woff2 (`assets/fonts/`), zero external CDN, declared in
`theme.json` Font Library + `assets/css/fonts.css`.

### Brand Device — the rose, everywhere
- **Rose-as-O:** the Signature move (rose bloom = the O in "R⊙se") extends across collections — Black R⊙se,
  L⊙ve — recolored per collection (black-chrome, crimson, gold, rose-gold), never redrawn.
- **Collection logos:** photoreal 3D star-rose emblems as transparent cutouts — Black Rose (glass star +
  black rose + heart base), Love Hurts (red star + red rose + broken heart). Hero/large marks.
- **Format law:** photoreal 3D marks ship as **transparent WebP**, NOT SVG (vector tracing flattens the
  glass/metal). High-res source, scaled down (retina-crisp, ~2–40KB). The full emblem reads at ≥ ~64px;
  below that use the simple **rose-only crop**, not the busy emblem.

## Design Principles
1. **Oakland-forged type, not European-luxe** — grotesque / athletic / stencil, never dainty serif.
2. **Dark canvas, disciplined palette, rare accent** — 60-30-10 by visual weight; the accent works because
   it's scarce.
3. **The garment and the photography lead** — type and chrome recede; "imagery hasn't earned it" restraint.
4. **One device across the house** — the rose recurs in every collection, recolored not redesigned.
5. **Accessible + performant by default** — AA contrast/focus, reduced-motion path, self-hosted woff2, WebP
   marks, budgeted above-the-fold media.
6. **Each collection keeps its register** — its own script, its own emotional voice; never mix collection
   quotes.

## Anti-slop guardrails (impeccable)
- No gradient text; no >1px colored side-stripe accents on cards/callouts.
- OKLCH color, neutrals tinted toward the brand hue; never pure `#000`/`#fff`.
- Fonts chosen against the brand-as-physical-object (poured concrete, port stencil, varsity patch) — not the
  training-default reflex list.
